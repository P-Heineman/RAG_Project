Workflow Highlights:
  • Query Rewriting: Leverages chat history to transform ambiguous follow-ups into standalone queries.
  • Router: An LLM-based decision point that directs queries to the most efficient retrieval path.
  • Semantic vs Structured: Distinguishes between "soft" document retrieval and precise data extraction from structured JSON.
  • Fallback: A safety mechanism that ensures an answer is provided even when initial high-quality thresholds aren't met.
